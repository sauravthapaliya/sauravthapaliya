About Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is a professional soccer player from Portugal. He is widely considered one of the greatest soccer players of all time and has won numerous awards and accolades throughout his career. Ronaldo currently plays for Manchester United in the English Premier League and is the captain of the Portuguese national team. He is known for his speed, dribbling skills, and scoring ability.

Cristiano Ronaldo records

Cristiano Ronaldo has achieved many accomplishments and records throughout his soccer career. Some of the most notable include: Most goals scored in the UEFA Champions League: Ronaldo has scored 138 goals in the Champions League, the most by any player in the history of the competition. Most goals scored in a single UEFA Champions League season: Ronaldo scored 17 goals in the 2013-2014 season, the most by any player in a single Champions League campaign. Most goals scored for a single club: Ronaldo scored 450 goals for Real Madrid in all competitions, the most by any player for a single club. Most goals scored in a single season for a single club: Ronaldo scored 69 goals for Real Madrid in all competitions in the 2014-2015 season, the most by any player for a single club in a single season. Most goals scored for the Portuguese national team: Ronaldo has scored 119 goals for the Portuguese national team, the most by any player in the team's history.
